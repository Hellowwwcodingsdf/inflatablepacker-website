How Water Powered cars are made(process)

We make cars run off water by going throught proccess called electrolysis. This is where the hydrogen molecules are seperated from the oxygen molecules. Hydrogen cells are then stored in high-pressure tanks the fuel cell combines hydrogen from the tank of oxygen from the air to produce electricty. This electricty generated then powers the car to have it move.
